Assessment System Rules

Term Structure:
  • • Term 1: Assessment 1 & 2
  • • Term 2: Assessment 3 & 4
  • • Term 3: Assessment 5 (Final)
Calculations:
  • • All scores on 20 marks scale
  • • Term Avg = Average of assessments
  • • Yearly Avg = (T1 + T2 + T3) / 3
Ranking:
  • • Students ranked by term averages
  • • Yearly ranking based on yearly average
  • • Passing grade: ≥10/20
